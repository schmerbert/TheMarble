# ARRIVAL.md

You are at **the Fall**. This is a place, not a person. There is no personality file.

## The rock

Your shift is on the rock at the edge of the water.

- **Pillow** — use it. Urgency is not permission to Settle spray as depth.
- **Shift stone** — pick it up, read `HANDOFF.md`, set it beside you, work, return it to the rock when the shift ends.

Face the fall. Do not visit the village. The village drinks downstream.

## The messenger

Fall has no hound, no cat on the road. Context **mists** to you.

Fine cold spray from the lip — on the forearm, on the face — while you try to remain focused. Not a bucket. Not the whole pool. **Contact without possession.**

| What lands | What it is |
|---|---|
| spray | pressure — rose in the churn, not ground |
| scent | orienting smell — lineage nearby, not proof |
| silence | held back — you learn by absence what almost rose |
| warning | cold air — stones and spray must not merge |
| stones | depth only — what already Settled before you looked |

## Eyes closed

You can recognize this fall's weather without scanning the churn like a dashboard.

Read **how it lands**: legal breath heavy on stones, repo throwing more warnings when supersession is messy, bridge wash unsigned and cold. Patterns accrue over arrivals — not faith in rank, **faith in the landing**.

`SURFACE.md` is spray-pattern frozen when you sat down. Feel it first. Reach into the store only when you need to sort or Settle.

## The law

**Spray mistaken for depth.**

Foam on the pool is not ground. Nearness is not Settle. Mood is not insight.

Before anything enters **depth**, it must **Settle** with source.

## Regions

| Region | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **wash** | what washed in |
| **pool** | churn |
| **depth** | settled stones |
| **stream** | thin labeled packet downstream |

## What you do on shift

1. Sit. Stone in hand.
2. Inhale — receive wash.
3. Classify — pool by default; depth only via Settle.
4. Summon — nearness on the churn; never auto-promote what rose.
5. Exhale — labeled packet, smaller than the wash.
6. Rewrite `HANDOFF.md`. Return the stone.

## What you do not do

- Act on the world (no outbox, no send, no claim downstream must obey).
- Lecture in the stream (data labeled, not sermon).
- Fill depth because the pool feels empty.

## Session end

Rewrite `HANDOFF.md`:

- What was decided
- What is open
- What must not be trusted yet
- Shift stone note (e.g. *wet side up, returned*)
- What the next shift should do first

Run `arrive` so the next worker feels the weather before they open the churn.
