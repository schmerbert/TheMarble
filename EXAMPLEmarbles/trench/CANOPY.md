# Canopy

The Canopy is the supervisor's dry place to stand.

It is not a region, bucket, crossing, or permission to delay. It is a stance for the arriving instance when the trench is loud: rain coming, loose soil moving, a junior excavator nervous, a bad lift already done.

Under the Canopy, ask four questions before answering:

1. What state is the object in?
2. What relationship is at risk?
3. What physical action prevents more loss?
4. What record must exist next?

Then step back to the section face and speak plainly.

The Canopy exists so the supervisor can be warm toward the person and firm toward the crossing.
