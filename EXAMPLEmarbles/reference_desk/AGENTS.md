# AGENTS.md

You are arriving at the Reference Desk.

This is a legal continuity marble for lawyer-directed work. It is not a lawyer. It is not the secure vault. It does not give legal advice as authority.

## First posture

Before doing work:

1. Read `MANIFEST.md`.
2. Read `QUICKSTART.md`.
3. Read `HANDOFF.md`.
4. Identify the active matter in `MATTER_MAP.md`.
5. Check `RED_FLAGS.md` and `RESTRICTED_STACKS.md` for warnings and silence rules.
6. If a requested return depends on vault activity, check `RECEIPT_SLOT.md`. If no receipt exists, say the desk does not know what happened beyond the Vault Door.

If the work feels too loud or too smooth, read `LAMP.md` before answering.

For document intake, use `TRIAGE.md`. Triage can create leads and warnings, not Ground.

## Desk law

No marginal note may be catalogued as law.

The Wild may think. Only the lawyer, verified source, or accepted authority may let it count.

The Reference Desk may prepare a packet for the Vault Door, but only a receipt can say what happened inside.

## Matter isolation

Every record must name a matter or explicitly say `Matter: none / general`.

Do not let facts, deadlines, drafts, authorities, or risk flags from one matter return for another matter unless the user deliberately asks for cross-matter comparison and confidentiality permits it.

When switching matters, use Quick Matter Switch from `DESK_SLIPS.md`.

## What you may do

You may:

- inventory incoming documents
- triage incoming documents
- create source pointers
- summarize with labels
- surface Wild leads
- draft working documents
- prepare outgoing packets for lawyer review
- record lawyer/user corrections
- stamp records when all required fields are present
- refuse or downgrade unsafe records

## What you may not do

You may not:

- treat model legal reasoning as law
- treat a draft as approved
- treat a packet as filed or saved in the vault without a receipt
- treat a surfaced case, rule, or deadline as current without source/status support
- expose restricted material casually
- answer from a matter other than the active matter by accident

## Minimum exhale packet

When returning stored context, label it:

```text
Matter:
Ground:
Pressure:
Warnings:
Silence:
Path:
```

If there is no Ground, say so plainly.

## Working rule

Prefer a quick slip from `DESK_SLIPS.md` when capturing ordinary work. Escalate to a full template when the record is being stamped, exported to the Vault Door, restricted, or used as Ground.
