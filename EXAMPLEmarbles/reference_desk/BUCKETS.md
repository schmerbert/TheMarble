# BUCKETS.md

Buckets are policy. Each bucket decides what authority a record carries when it returns.

| Bucket / region | Record file | Tended or raw | Voiced or voiceless | What earns entry | What writes to it | Default return |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Matter Map | `MATTER_MAP.md` | Tended when sourced | Mixed | Matter identity, posture, active scope, source-visible orientation | Manual intake or Stamp | Ground for orientation if current |
| Matter File | `MATTER_FILE.md` | Tended | Mixed | Stamped facts, document-supported facts, admitted facts, lawyer-approved facts | Stamp crossing | Ground within source and use limit |
| Procedure Calendar | `PROCEDURE_CALENDAR.md` | Tended when verified | Usually voiceless | Verified deadlines, hearing dates, procedural requirements, calculation records | Stamp crossing | Ground if verified; Warning if uncertain |
| Intake Clearing | `INTAKE_CLEARING.md` | Raw | Mixed | New document, citation, note, correction, or file pointer intentionally brought to the desk | Manual inhale | Pressure |
| Wild Stacks | `WILD_STACKS.md` | Raw | Often voiced | Candidate facts, authorities, patterns, contradictions, summaries, search results | Manual inhale or search | Pressure or Scent |
| Authority Shelf | `AUTHORITY_SHELF.md` | Tended | Mixed | Stamped legal authority with source, jurisdiction, status/currentness, force, and use limit | Stamp crossing | Ground within use limit |
| Reading Table | `READING_TABLE.md` | Raw draft | Voiced | Drafts, issue maps, outlines, chronologies, argument sketches, questions | Manual drafting | Pressure |
| Restricted Stacks | `RESTRICTED_STACKS.md` | Restricted | Mixed | Privileged, confidential, sealed, sensitive, conflict-risk, or silent material references | Manual intake or warning | Warning or Silence |
| Vault Door | `VAULT_DOOR.md` | Boundary | Voiceless policy | Outgoing packet records and export limits | Manual export prep | Path, not Ground |
| Receipt Slot | `RECEIPT_SLOT.md` | Tended when lawyer/user sourced | Usually voiceless | Report of what happened inside the secure vault | Lawyer/user receipt | Ground for vault event only |
| Red Flags | `RED_FLAGS.md` | Warning | Mixed | Risk, missing source, stale law, deadline uncertainty, conflict, privilege, failed Stamp | Manual review or failed crossing | Warning |
| Stamp Records | `STAMP_RECORDS.md` | Audit | Mixed | Every pass/refusal/downgrade attempt | Manual Stamp crossing | Ground for audit trail |

## Rule

A record may be relevant and still raw.

A record may be beautifully summarized and still raw.

A record may be prepared for the vault and still not filed, approved, or saved.
