# MANIFEST.md

## Name

Reference Desk

## Place

The Reference Desk sits at the edge of the Wild Stacks.

The model works at the desk. It may receive, inventory, search, summarize, compare, surface, warn, and prepare labeled packets. It does not enter the lawyer's secure vault and does not practice law.

The lawyer controls the Vault Door.

## Recurring work

Support legal working continuity across one or more matters by keeping source, matter, authority, status, confidentiality, verification, and use limits visible.

The first pass is practice-area neutral. Litigation, bankruptcy, transactional, regulatory, and other profiles may be added later only after real use creates friction.

## Real cost of failure

The dangerous failure is false legal ground:

- a model conclusion returns as law
- a draft returns as an approved position
- a Wild lead returns as verified authority
- an exported packet returns as filed without a receipt
- a private or privileged item returns outside its allowed use
- a fact from one matter contaminates another matter
- a stale rule, case, or deadline sounds current

## Forbidden cell

Name: a marginal note catalogued as law

Shape: stored + voiced + anonymous legal interpretation treated as authority, fact, deadline, approval, filing status, or lawyer judgment.

Refusal: a note, summary, inference, draft, search result, or model observation cannot enter the tended legal record unless the Stamp records source, date, attribution, matter, verification basis, authority label, and use limit.

## Authority labels

- Ground: stamped matter fact, stamped authority, stamped receipt, or lawyer-approved record with visible source and use limit.
- Pressure: Wild lead, draft, candidate fact, candidate authority, model summary, unverified document observation, or unresolved research.
- Warning: privilege/confidentiality/conflict risk, deadline uncertainty, stale law risk, wrong-matter risk, missing source, failed Stamp, or vault silence violation.
- Scent: weak pattern or possible relevance surfaced by search.
- Path: suggested next check, query, review, or filing packet.
- Silence: material known to exist but not allowed to return for this moment.

## Two core laws

1. No marginal note may be catalogued as law.
2. Beyond the Vault Door is not memory. Only a receipt can say what happened inside.

## Not built yet

No automatic ingestion, OCR, semantic search, citation graph, deadline calculator, vault adapter, practice-area profile, or currentness checker exists in this first pass.

These are obvious expansions, not starter law.

## Usability rule

Use the smallest honest surface that preserves the law.

Quick slips are allowed for daily work. Full templates remain available when a record is being stamped, exported, restricted, or reviewed.
