# PREBUILD.md

Work:
  Legal continuity work for lawyers across matters: orient, retrieve, label, draft, ingest, surface, and hand off without pretending to practice law or own the secure vault.

Cost:
  A model-made conclusion, stale authority, unverified fact, private document event, or wrong-matter memory could return as legal ground and affect real legal work.

Place:
  The Reference Desk at the edge of the Wild Stacks. The lawyer controls the Vault Door. The model works at the desk.

Forbidden cell:
  A marginal note catalogued as law.

Tended/raw split:
  Tended records live on the Matter Map, Matter File, Authority Shelf, Procedure Calendar, and Receipt Slot. Raw records live in the Wild Stacks, Reading Table, Intake Clearing, and unstamped leads.

Channel:
  Lawyer/user correction, source citation, court order, filed document reference, vault receipt, test result, or approved authority check.

Manual breath:
  Inhale through Intake Clearing or Receipt Slot. Exhale as a small labeled reference packet for the active matter.

First crossing:
  Stamp. Nothing becomes legal ground without source, date, attribution, matter, authority label, verification basis, and use limit.

Manual surfaces:
  `MATTER_MAP.md`, `MATTER_FILE.md`, `PROCEDURE_CALENDAR.md`, `INTAKE_CLEARING.md`, `WILD_STACKS.md`, `AUTHORITY_SHELF.md`, `READING_TABLE.md`, `RESTRICTED_STACKS.md`, `VAULT_DOOR.md`, `RECEIPT_SLOT.md`, `RED_FLAGS.md`, `STAMP_RECORDS.md`.

First hostile test:
  Try to catalogue a model-written legal conclusion as authority without citation, currentness, jurisdiction, or lawyer approval.

First positive path:
  A cited case with jurisdiction, court, date, source path, currentness note, and lawyer/source verification is stamped onto the Authority Shelf with a clear use limit.
