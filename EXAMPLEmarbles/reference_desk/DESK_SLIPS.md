# DESK_SLIPS.md

Quick slips are short working cards.

They reduce accidental friction without weakening the law. Use them for ordinary work, then expand into full templates when needed.

## Expansion rule

Expand a quick slip into the full bucket template when:

- it will be relied on as Ground
- it crosses Stamp
- it contains restricted material
- it goes to or returns from the Vault Door
- it affects a deadline, filing, or procedural requirement
- a future reviewer needs a complete audit trail
